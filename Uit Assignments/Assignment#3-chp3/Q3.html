Muffin Recipe

A basic recipe. You can add pretty much anythingh to it

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 200gm caster sugar
  • 250ml milk
  • 125ml vegetable oil
  • 400gm plain flour
  • 3tbs baking powder
  • 1tbs salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to gas mark 6 or a medium temperature.Line muffin tray with papper muffin cases.
  2. Beat the eggs in a making bowl and then mix the oil and milk. Add the sugar and whisk until dissolved
  3. Shift in the dry ingredients:flour,baking powder,salt and mix until it smooth's.NB: Add extra ingridients
  4. Fill muffin casses TWO THIRDS full (This is imp for perfect size muffins) and bake the middle of the oven for 25 minutes approx
  5. Leave to cool and Enjoy!!!
